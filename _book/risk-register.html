CA-003 Larger contracts may be broken into multiple contracts. Accept High High
CA-004 The type of contract, Design/Build or Design/Bid/Build, can affect timeline and cost. Accept Moderate Low

EN-001 The assumption made during the feasibility study was the site could be used for construction with some remediation. Environmental site investigation may result in unusable site or high cost. Accept & Mitigate Moderate Moderate
